Who We Are

About the School

Londiani Christian Academy is a distinguished private mixed day and boarding institution committed to holistic education from Kindergarten through Junior School. We provide a secure, inspiring, and inclusive setting where every learner is encouraged to discover purpose and potential.

With small class sizes, technology-enhanced learning, and a strong partnership with parents, we deliver high-quality academic and personal development outcomes.

Vision

To be Kenya's benchmark private school for transformational, future-ready education.

Mission

To cultivate excellence, integrity, and creativity through learner-centered teaching and values-based leadership.

Core Values

  • Academic Excellence
  • Integrity & Respect
  • Innovation
  • Discipline
  • Community Service
